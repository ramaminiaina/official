About Me

Based in South Africa with a background in Physics and astronomy, my ambition is to leverage science and technology for a meaningful impact in Africa. Professionally, I aim to become an expert data scientist, astronomer, and professor. Motivated by the shortage of astronomy professionals in my country, I aspire to contribute significantly to the field, cultivate the next generation of astronomers, and establish an institution addressing educational gaps in my country.

In my free time, I enjoy playing the piano or organ, especially for my church community. Witnessing people sing along with the music brings me great joy. While the organ holds a special place in my heart, I am motivated to explore various musical instruments.

Additionally, I prioritize a healthy lifestyle with a dedicated one-hour run five days a week. Running serves as both a physical outlet and a source of mental clarity, reinforcing discipline and well-being in my daily routine.
